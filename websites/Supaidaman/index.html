Spider-Man (Peter Parker) is a superhero created by writer-editor Stan Lee and artist Steve Ditko.
Peter Parker has appeared in a number of comic books, films, and television shows over the years.
Different versions of the character have been created over time, including an alternate universe
Version of "Peter Parker" or entirely new Spider-Man characters such as "Miles Morales."
Spider-Man has been portrayed by a number of famous actors, including
Tobey Maguire, Andrew Garfield, Tom Holland, Chris Pine, and Jake Johnson.








  BACKGROUND:

  Peter Parker, commonly known as Spider-Man, is a full-time superhero most recognized for his web-slinging and wall-crawling abilities.
  Peter was raised by his aunt May and uncle Ben as an orphaned child. Peter was bitten by a stray radioactive spider,
  And as a result of the bite, he now has enhanced athletic abilities as well as the ability to attach to walls and ceilings.
  He creates web-shooters, a device that allows him to shoot artificial webbing.
